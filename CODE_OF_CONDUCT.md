# Code of Conduct

UK Web Marketing (operated by TicketWave HQ Ltd, Companies House no. [17143167](https://find-and-update.company-information.service.gov.uk/company/17143167)) maintains the repositories under the [`ukwebmarketing`](https://github.com/ukwebmarketing) GitHub organisation as a professional working environment.

## Our standards

- **Be respectful.** Disagree with ideas, never with people. Critique work, not the worker.
- **Be specific.** Reports of a bug, a regression, or a security issue should be reproducible.
- **Be patient.** Maintainers are working engineers, not a help-desk. Replies happen within UK business hours.
- **Be honest.** Do not misrepresent affiliations, credentials, or test results.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks of any kind
- Publishing private information without consent
- Sustained disruption of issue threads, pull requests, or discussions
- Submitting deliberately misleading bug reports or proof-of-concept exploits
- Any conduct that would be unlawful under UK law

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behaviour that breaches this Code of Conduct in a repository under this organisation, contact:

- **General conduct**: [hello@ukwebmarketing.com](mailto:hello@ukwebmarketing.com)
- **Security disclosures**: [security@ukwebmarketing.com](mailto:security@ukwebmarketing.com)

All reports are reviewed by a director of TicketWave HQ Ltd. We acknowledge reports within one UK business day.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, lock threads, block contributors, or take any action reasonably required to keep the repositories productive. Repeated or serious breaches result in a permanent ban from the organisation.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to every repository under the `ukwebmarketing` GitHub organisation, every issue, every pull request, every discussion, and every channel listed on the [public org profile](https://github.com/ukwebmarketing).
