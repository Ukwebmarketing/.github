# Support

Most repositories under the [`ukwebmarketing`](https://github.com/ukwebmarketing) GitHub organisation are private client repositories. If you are a UK Web Marketing client, contact us through your usual channel — email, WhatsApp, or the audit form on [ukwebmarketing.com](https://ukwebmarketing.com).

For everyone else, route by topic:

| Topic | Where to go |
|---|---|
| **General enquiries** | [hello@ukwebmarketing.com](mailto:hello@ukwebmarketing.com) |
| **Free site audit** | [ukwebmarketing.com/audit](https://ukwebmarketing.com/audit) |
| **Pricing and plans** | [ukwebmarketing.com/pricing](https://ukwebmarketing.com/pricing) |
| **Existing customer support** | reply to the most recent thread with your engineer, or email [hello@ukwebmarketing.com](mailto:hello@ukwebmarketing.com) |
| **WhatsApp (preferred for urgent client matters)** | [+44 7553 070841](https://wa.me/447553070841) |
| **Security disclosures** | [security@ukwebmarketing.com](mailto:security@ukwebmarketing.com) — see [SECURITY.md](./SECURITY.md) |
| **Code of Conduct concerns** | [hello@ukwebmarketing.com](mailto:hello@ukwebmarketing.com) — see [CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md](./CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md) |
| **Press and partnerships** | [hello@ukwebmarketing.com](mailto:hello@ukwebmarketing.com) |

## Response times

- Email and WhatsApp: same UK business day for existing clients; within one UK business day otherwise
- Security disclosures: acknowledged within 24 hours; fix or written explanation within 7 days
- GitHub issues on public repos: within one UK business week

## What we cannot help with through this org

- Hosting or DNS issues for third-party agencies' sites
- Free debugging of code we did not write
- WordPress or PHP support (we do not ship either)

If you are unsure which channel applies, default to [hello@ukwebmarketing.com](mailto:hello@ukwebmarketing.com) — we route from there.
